Welcome to Alabama Weather

Alabama has a subtropical climate, with hot, humid summers and mild winters. North Alabama, in particular, experiences a mix of weather patterns due to its location. From scorching summers to the occasional snowfall, the weather here is always interesting. This site provides real-time weather updates and forecasts for cities in North Alabama and beyond.
